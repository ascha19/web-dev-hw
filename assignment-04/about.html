About Amanda Lovelace

Amanada Lovelace is an American poet. She was named Goodreads Poet of the Year and she's best known
for her series Women Are Some Kind of Magic. She's also written works such as the series Things That
H(a)unt and the novel His Hideous Heart: 13 of Edgar Allen Poe's Most Unsettling Tales Reimagined. Her
newest work, "Break Your Glass Slippers," which is the first installment of her new series You are Your Own
Fairy Tale, was released two days ago, on March 17th of 2020.
