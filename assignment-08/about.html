Story Behind the Squishy

Squishy Guide Contact Info

Who

My name is Avery and I'm a Media Design student. This site and project, despite the overall personal fun I had with it, were created as an assignment for my Web Design class.

What

It's no surprise that this squishy is modeled after the one and only Marvel's Captain America shield. However, while I used the regular red, white, and blue fabrics, by all means take creative liberty and use some wild colors instead. This design is also a work-in-progress, and my test project is by no means perfect.

When

The squishy was designed and created in a span of a few hours, one or two days ago; so somewhere around June 3rd and 5th, 2020. The site itself is still actively being worked on created as I'm typing this, at about 15 minutes to 10 pm on a Sunday.

Where

Both the squishy and site were/are being created within my humble abode, primarily in my art workspace or the living room while watching weird movies and weirder reality TV.

Why

Superheroes, especially those of the Marvel universe, have been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. I own a lot of Marvel merch, including Pop! figures, comics, and clothes. I've drawn some of the characters as well, so I decided to make something new to add to my collection. I've made other pillow/squishy-style pieces in the past, so this is nothing new.
